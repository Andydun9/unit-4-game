Car Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are cars below. By clicking on a car you will add a specific amount of point to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each car is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game start, the game will change the values of each car.
